Mayleemouse


While looking around on DeviantArt for art for this project, I rediscovered Mayleemouse's work from the days of Tumblr! The ones I've featured reminded me a lot of Steven Universe's creator - Rebecca Sugar! I've always loved their fashion illustrations, but I got to say their flora pieces are my absolute favorite!
